Professional Overview

▼

💼 Experience & Expertise

20+ years in Data Science and Decision Automation in Financial Services, with leadership experience across multiple organisations including the CBA, Zip BNPL, Slice, and ING.

🎯 Strategic Leadership

Driving business value through strategic technical leadership and innovation, with expertise in architecting scalable, automated solutions that deliver measurable outcomes.

📊 Risk Management Excellence

Certified Practising Risk Manager (CPRM®) — a professional credential awarded by the Risk Management Institute of Australasia (RMIA) for experienced practitioners demonstrating strategic risk oversight, governance, and organisational resilience competencies.

💳 Credit Risk Specialisation

Deep expertise in end-to-end credit risk modelling — IFRS9 expected credit loss, PD/LGD/EAD model development, regulatory capital under the Internal Ratings-Based (IRB) approach, and model validation. Led the IRB model development that secured APRA's approval for ING Bank Australia to use internal models to calculate credit risk capital requirements, with parallel engagement on ECB IRB standards.

📰 In the News
APRA approves ING Bank Australia Limited to use internal models to calculate credit risk capital requirements — Australian Prudential Regulation Authority

🚀 Innovation & Automation

Expert in end-to-end credit lifecycle decisioning automation: underwriting, limit management, early warning systems, collection optimisation, and recovery strategies.

🏆 2026 Data Science HiPo Talent Program

Selected as one of 16 participants globally in CBA's Data Science High-Potential Talent Program (April – November 2026), designed to accelerate Senior Managers into Chapter Area Lead and Principal Data Scientist roles. Programme includes leadership assessments, workshops, mentoring, and a group capstone project.
